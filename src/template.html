About me

I am a seasoned account manager with several years of work experience under my belt. In addition to my account manager position, I am a Web Designer/ Developer. I enjoy designing Websites, Landing Pages, and Blogs with Adobe Xd, Photoshop and Illustrator, and Figma. To develop the websites, I use HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, JavaScript, and soon I’ll be able to add React to the list of developing tools.

Aside from my passion for designing and developing, I enjoy learning new things that will help me in design and development. When I'm not in school, majoring in Digital & Social Media Marketing, I enjoy taking online courses in web development. My goal is to continue to add more tools to my webdev arsenal as possible. I plan to continue to learn for as long as I can.

When I'm not working on my design and development skills, I am a big sports fan and have begun to collect sportscards as a hobby. From Baseball to Football to Basketball, I have started to collect standard and rare collectible cards.
